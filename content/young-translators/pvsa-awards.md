# Presidential Volunteer Service Awards

Students in the Young Translators program can earn the prestigious Presidential Volunteer Service Award (PVSA) for their community service hours.

## What is PVSA?

The Presidential Volunteer Service Award recognizes Americans of all ages who have volunteered significant amounts of their time to serve their communities and their country.

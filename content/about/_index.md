# About EETHAL Foundation

**EETHAL (ஈதல்)** means "giving" in Tamil. It represents our core value of giving back to the community through **E**ducation and **E**mpowerment **TH**rough **A**pplied **L**anguages.

## Our Mission

EETHAL Foundation is dedicated to preserving the Tamil language and culture while empowering the next generation through education and service.

## What We Do

We provide educational programs and resources that:
- Preserve Tamil language and culture for future generations
- Develop leadership skills through community service
- Connect Tamil-American youth with their heritage
- Serve the Tamil-speaking community
